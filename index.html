Chloe the Akita

Meet my dog!

This is Chloe, known affectionally as Chloebeary or bear bear! She is an Akita my family adopted six years ago when we were living in Japan. She is a chronic couch potato and often antisocial, but so cute and loveable!

Her Likes and Dislikes

She is happiest when:

- walking in nature
- sunbathing
- sleeping the day away
and most of all:
- devouring her treats

Her unhappiest is when:

- Bishop, my other dog, wants to play with her
- my family won't share our food with her
- her sleep is disturbed
- getting baths
