Chloe the Akita

Meet my dog!

This is Chloe, known affectionally as Chloebeary or bear bear :) She is a Japanese Akita my family adopted six years ago when we were living in Japan. Her looks and her personality are sometimes more like a cat than a dog. She is a chronic couch potato, hiding away to sleep most the day, and even when she's awake she's often antisocial or nonchalant. Much like a cat, her don't-care attitude makes it even nicer when you get a chance to see her sweet side :)

Her Likes and Dislikes

Despite her personality, her interests are still very true to the typical dog!

She is happiest when:

- walking in nature
- sunbathing
- sleeping the day away
and most of all:
- devouring her treats

She is a menace, too smart for her own good, when it comes to food. Recently, she has learned how to get closed bags unzipped to rummage for food whenever we leave her home.

Her unhappiest is when:

- Bishop, my other dog, wants to play with her
- my family won't share our food with her
- her sleep is disturbed
- getting a bath

Whenever she is annoyed-which is often-her response is always a grumbling sound like an angry bear. It can scare people when they don't know her, but in reality she is less dangerous than a teddy bear.
